The Functionally, Visualization tool is divided into 5 components. These components are

  • the sidebar on left side
  • the map
  • the toolbar at the top of the map and
  • the status bar at the bottom
  • the header at the top

The Sidebar

The Sidebar has three tabs, map elements including layers and Legend, search forms, and subscription forms.

Map Elements

The map Elements, located as a first tab of the sidebar, is composed of the Layers list and the Legend.

The Layers Panel is composed of Base Maps and Overlays. By default, when the system loads for the first time, the layer that will be loaded in the map is the latest captured fire point. The other layers such as the last 48 hrs fire points, conservation areas, and towns can be viewed by checking on the checkbox. More layers will be loaded when you make search using the search forms located under the Search tab. All layers that are loaded can be shown and hidden from the map and the legend panel by using the checkboxes next to each checkboxes.

The Legend Panel shows the legend of the loaded data on the map. When more than one legends are shown, you might need to use the scrollbar to see the hidden legends.

The fire data is categorized into 4 groups based on their their brightness (Kelvin) value.

  • Low Intensity Fire - Less than 310
  • Medium Intensity Fire - 310.1 - 340.9
  • High Intensity Fire - 341.0 - 360.9
  • Extreme Intensity Fire - Above 361.0

Search

The Search tab, enables you to search fire data by countries, affected and nearby conservation areas and nearby towns. In addition, you can select to view a heat map instead of point map for range and custom search.

The search is divided into 3 types namely, single search, range search and custom search.

Single Search – is used to find data on a specific date.

Range Search – is used to find data in between two dates.

Custom Search – is used to search for data for a specific year, month or date over the entire record.

Each search types can be shown and hidden by click on the plus and minus buttons located in the title bar of each search types.

After making your search filter, when you click on the "Search" button, you will see the search result appearing on the map.

Subscribe

The subscribe tab is composed of subscribe form that enable you sign up for alerts in case fire happens in area of your interest.

The Map

The Map panel, located at the center of your screen, displays the all layers of the visualization tool. There are there different tools in the top part of the map.

In the top left, there are the zoom in and out buttons. In the right side, there is an additional layer selection tool which is commonly known as the layer switcher. At the bottom right corner, there is a scale that shows the ratio of distance of the visualization tool and on the ground. Finally, there is a small tooltip that displays the coordinates (Longitude / Latitude) of your mouse hover on the map.

Toolbar

The Toolbar, located above the Map Panel, has option to control the map, and read more about the tool. Hover your mouse cursor on each tool buttons to read what they do.

Status Bar

The status bar displays data last update information.
